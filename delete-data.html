Delete Your Prayle Data

Prayle stores all of your data locally on your device. No personal data is stored on any server. This means you are always in full control of your data.

To delete all your data

Simply uninstall Prayle from your device. This will permanently remove all locally stored app data including notes, journal entries, goals, commitments, checklists, and any other content you have created.

To delete specific data

You can delete individual items directly within the app at any time. Items can be archived and permanently deleted from the Archive screen.

In-app purchase data

If you have made a purchase through Google Play, that transaction data is managed by Google. To request deletion of purchase records please visit Google Play Support.
